My Journey!

MBA (2017-2019)

While pursuing MBA at Welingkar Institute of Management in Bangalore, I came across these buzz words of new data driven world like Business Analytics, AI, Data Science etc. From there I learned how businesses are optimizing their operations and leveraging this tool in enhancing their productivity in every aspect. I learned basic business statistics and how it is used to understand population from given data or sample.

Internships

Paisool (INBE Technology Pvt Ltd) - Out of curiosity I applied for the Data Analyst Internship in one of Bangalore based grocery start up Paisool during my MBA. There I worked with CTO on optimizing their routes in delivery of products to grocery stores and developed a Dashboard for Sales Team to analyze their performance. I learned advance Excel and got exposed to Python Programming Language, SQL and other tools like SPSS.

Stanza Living –At last year of my MBA after getting campus placement I did an Internship in India’s one of fastest growing start up and largest managed - accommodation space provider to students and working professionals in India. There I worked with Procurement Team where they were setting up their supply chain for providing services in Hostel and material to new developments in Tier 2 cities. Everything was Data Driven where our objective was to Minimize Cost and Maximize Quality. There I gained people’s skill and how to negotiate with vendors after analyzing information.

Work Experience (2019 – 2021)

ICICI Bank – Deputy Manager, Trade Finance

I was assigned as a Trade Finance Manager to the bunch of Branches in Central Delhi. My role was to enhance and manage Trade Banking Business in the region and this market was highly competitive as other private sector banks were also active. I understood bank was focusing on using intelligence and digitization to create their USP in the Market. ICICI Bank came out with Trade Online Feature which was first digital banking service offered by any bank in India in Trade Finance space. In Trade Business my department was focusing on Small Medium Enterprises (SMEs) segment which is changing rapidly after e-commerce. SMEs segment is adapting to digitization and trade online made huge success as it was making banking easy for Importer and Exporters both. Reports from Business Intelligence Unit helped us in understanding the market and how to and what to pitch to different segments of customers. I also trained branch employees on trade products and benefits of Trade Online. As, Trade Online was becoming popular and we started getting interesting data on customers behavior in using Banking Services. As remittances are international compliance becomes challenging but with digitization it became very easy for us to maintain and track every transaction smoothly.

Advance Management Program in Business Analytics, Indian School of Business, Hyderabad. (Present)

I got selected in ISB’s flagship Data Science Program and started pursuing it. While working I always understood the importance of Data in every process of Business. Data is the solution for simple to complex problems. While studying here I understood the Lifecycle of Data Science and got skilled in Python, SQL, Power BI and many other tools which are necessary in understanding and working on any Data Science Project. From Day 1 my focus was to learn and get exposed with full stack Data Science Lifecycle right from identifying problem to Production and further maintenance and improvement.
